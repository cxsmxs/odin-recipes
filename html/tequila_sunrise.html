Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz. Lime Juice (Fresh-Squeezed Preferred)
  • 4 oz. Orange Juice
  • 0.5 oz Grenadine
  • Orange Slice (for Garnish)
  • Maraschino Cherry (for Garnish)
  • Ice

Barware

  • Collins Glass
  • Stirring Spoon

Steps

  1. Add ice into the Collins Glass.
  2. Combine liquids in the glass (Tequila, Lime Juice, Orange Juice)
  3. Stir using the stirring spoon until all liquids are generously combined.
  4. Hold stirring spoon against glass at an angle. Pour Grenadine down spoon. Do not stir.
  5. Top with orange slice and cherry for garnish.
