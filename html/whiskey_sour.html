Whiskey Sour

A concoction known for its simplicity and humble nature. A true elixir for distinguished persons.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Bourbon Whiskey (the higher quality, the better)
  • 0.75 oz Lemon Juice
  • 0.75 oz. Simple Syrup
  • Orange Peel (for Garnish)
  • Maraschino Cherry (for Garnish)
  • Ice

Barware

  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Hawthorne Strainer
  • Rocks Glass or Goblet-Style Glass

Steps

  1. Combine liquids (simple syrup, lemon juice, whiskey) into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice to shaker, cover, and shake furiously.
  3. Strain shaken cocktail through strainer into glassware filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with cherry and orange slice.
